Bengal Cats

Historical Information

Bengal cats are a hybrid cat breed and they have only been around for about two centuries! In fact, in the early 1900s cat breeders crossed domestic felines with Asian leopard cats that are domestic to Southeast Asia.

Behavior and Qualities

Bengal cats are very sweet and loving towards people. Although, they can be quite opininative; they attach closely to people as a loyal friend. The bengal cat is very observitative of its surroundings and perches on high surfaces. As a large, they are very athletic and needs to run, jump and romp to be contented!

Pet Guide to Bengal Cats

  1. Temperament: Intelligent, vocal and lively.
  2. Social/Attention Needs: High.
  3. Average Life Span: 10 to 15 years.
  4. Coat length: Short.
  5. Grooming required: Moderate.

Bengal Cats Diet

  • Meats with less bones (fish, chicken, beef)
  • Eggs or day old chicks
  • Canned and wet bengal cat specified food
